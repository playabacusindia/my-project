The Franchise Process

Your journey from application to a successful launch.

Submit Your Application

Fill out our simple online application form to express your interest. We review your details to understand your background, location preferences, and business goals. This is the first step towards joining our growing network of educational entrepreneurs.

We value transparency and promise a straightforward process from day one. Once you apply, our team will get back to you quickly to discuss the next steps and see if we are a good fit for each other.

Discover Our Model

Once your application is shortlisted, we invite you to an orientation session. Here, we walk you through our proven business model, our unique curriculum, and exactly what it takes to run a successful center.

  • Understand our vision and teaching methods
  • Get a clear picture of the investment and expected returns
  • Meet our core team and get all your questions answered
Formalizing Our Partnership

After a mutual agreement, we move forward with the formal paperwork. We keep our franchise agreement clear and easy to understand, outlining the responsibilities and the support provided from both sides.

Signing this document officially makes you a part of the IPA family. It secures your exclusive territory, ensuring you have the space to grow your center without internal competition. We make sure you are fully comfortable before signing on the dotted line.

Equipping You for Success

We believe your success is our success. That's why we provide comprehensive training for you and your staff.

You will receive hands-on guidance covering all aspects of running a center.

  • Trainer Certification: Intensive programs on abacus techniques and classroom management.
  • Center Operations: Clear guidelines on running daily activities smoothly.
  • Marketing Materials: We share brochures, digital assets, and local promotion strategies.
  • Parent Counseling: Training on how to effectively communicate with parents and convert inquiries.

We make sure you feel completely confident before you open your doors to your first batch of students.

Opening Your Doors

The big day! We help you plan a strong opening for your center to attract the community's attention.

A successful launch sets the tone for your center's future growth.

  • Inauguration Planning: Support with event ideas to create local buzz.
  • Initial Enrollment: Strategies to attract your very first batch of students.
  • Software Setup: Getting your center management system up and running smoothly.

Our team stays closely connected with you during the launch phase to ensure everything goes off without a hitch.

Continuous Growth Together

Our relationship doesn't end at the launch. We provide continuous support as your business scales.

We are always working in the background to bring you better tools and opportunities.

  • Regular Workshops: Advanced training sessions for your staff to keep skills sharp.
  • Curriculum Updates: Access to the latest teaching materials and methods.
  • Business Reviews: Periodic check-ins to help you improve enrollment and daily operations.
  • National Events: Opportunities for your students to participate in state and national level competitions.

You are never alone in this journey. We are always just a call away to help you overcome challenges and celebrate your wins.

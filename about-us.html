About Us

Ideal Play Abacus India Private Limited

Ideal Play Abacus India (IPA) was founded in 2003 with a vision to revolutionize brain and math skills in children using the power of the abacus.

Today, we are proud to be a global leader in skill-based education, offering specialized programs like Abacus Maths, Abacus Mastermind, and Arka Kids. With a vast network of 1500+ centers and over 5,00,000+ students trained, we continue to shape young minds across India and abroad.

  • One of the primary institutions founded by an all-women entrepreneur team in 2003, Ideal Play Abacus India Private Limited (IPA) has grown into a leading provider of abacus education.
  • With over 1500+ centers in India, 2000+ trained teachers, and more than 5,00,000 enthusiastic students, IPA is at the forefront of abacus maths and abacus mastermind training.

Mrs. Shaarada K.Sriram

MANAGING DIRECTOR

My Self

A qualified Chartered Accountant and Cost Accountant, she is the Managing Director of Ideal Play Abacus India Pvt Ltd. Academically, she ranked All India 10th in Chartered Accountancy, was a gold medalist in ICWA (Institute of Cost and Works Accountants of India), and earned a gold medal in her Bachelors degree (B.Com). She also secured State 2nd position in the 12th Standard School Leaving Exam. Additionally, she was named the Best Outgoing Student of Stella Maris College and represented India at the Commonwealth Games Closing Ceremony in New Zealand.

She began her career as a Funds Manager at Unit Trust of India and ventured into the abacus business 16 years ago. With her expertise in business affairs and deep knowledge of the abacus education sector, she successfully leads the company, providing quality abacus maths and abacus mastermind programs across India and beyond.

Vision

Our vision is to help young minds unlock their potential through abacus learning, building strong abacus maths and mental skills for success.


More about us

Ideal Play Abacus offers a unique two-tier program:

  • Elementary program for children aged 4-6.

  • Regular program for children aged 7-12.

We also conduct state-level and national-level competitions, where distinguished guests like Ms. Kanimozhi (MP) and Mr. G.K. Vasan (Union Minister) have motivated our students. Our programs focus on building strong abacus maths and mental skills for young learners.

About Us

Ideal Play Abacus India Private Limited

Ideal Play Abacus India (IPA) was founded in 2003 with a vision to revolutionize brain and math skills in children using the power of the abacus.

Today, we are proud to be a global leader in skill-based education, offering specialized programs like Abacus Maths, Abacus Mastermind, and Arka Kids. With a vast network of 1500+ centers and over 5,00,000+ students trained, we continue to shape young minds across India and abroad.

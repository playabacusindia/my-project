Designed for young learners, this course ensures they acquire the necessary skills to speak, read, and write confidently.

What does phonics mean?

Phonics is a method of teaching reading that emphasizes the relationship between letters and sounds, essentially helping children learn how to decode words by connecting letters to sounds.

There are 44 sounds

We blend sounds together to help us read (build words up).

We segment words into sounds to help us spell (break words down).
